Problems completing a trade

If you're a seller, and you need a hand getting in touch with the buyer – we can help.

Get in touch with us, and we’ll work with you and the seller to try and resolve the issue. By submitting a dispute about a trade, you agree to us contacting the seller on your behalf. Depending on the nature of your dispute, you may be copied into the initial correspondence. Trade Me doesn’t have the legal standing to force the seller to complete the trade. If you believe the trade should go ahead – reach out to the Disputes Tribunal for help.
