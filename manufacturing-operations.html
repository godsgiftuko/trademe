Warehouse Assistant

Wurth NZ Ltd

Listed yesterday | Be an early applicant
Be an early applicant
Full time hours, Monday to Friday
Location Christchurch City, Canterbury
Job type Full time
Duration Permanent

Description

A great opportunity has come up for a hardworking and reliable person to assist with our successful and family orientated warehouse to cover over the peak period.

About the role: We are looking for an experienced warehouse person who takes pride in providing top service. Duties will include accurately picking and packing customer orders and supporting the inwards goods department.

About you: We are looking for a team player with a can-do attitude and friendly manner.

You will also have:

  • Inwards good experience preferred but not essential
  • Be physically fit
  • Organised and the ability to work unsupervised
  • Reliable with a strong work ethic
  • A team player
  • Health & Safety conscious
  • SAP experience is desirable but not compulsory
