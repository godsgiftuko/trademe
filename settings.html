Privacy options

We care about your privacy and we are committed to being transparent with how your personal information is used and disclosed. In our privacy policy, we commit to giving you the ability to change your privacy options. More information is available on our ‘Info about your privacy options’ page.

Changes to your privacy options may take up to 24 hours to take effect.

When opted in, Trade Me can use and disclose your information:

to show me relevant third-party advertising to advertise its services to me off-site to trusted third parties using Trade Me tailored products, subject to any customisable settings to entities fully or partly owned by Trade Me that provide Trade Me affiliated services. When opted out, advertising may be less relevant and some functionality of Trade Me affiliated services may be reduced. Whether you’re opted in or out, any customisable settings you have selected for Tailored Products (or any of our other services) will prevail.
